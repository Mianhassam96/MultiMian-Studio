## 6. DISCOVERY CALL FRAMEWORK

### PRE-CALL PREPARATION
- Review their website (if they have one)
- Check their LinkedIn/social media
- Understand their industry
- Prepare relevant case studies
- Have calendar ready for next steps

---

### CALL STRUCTURE (30 MINUTES)

**PART 1: INTRODUCTION (3 minutes)**
- Thank them for their time
- Brief intro about MultiMian
- Set agenda for the call
- Ask permission to take notes

**Script:**
"Thanks for taking the time to chat today, [Name]. I'm Mian Hassam from MultiMian. We specialize in building websites and web applications that drive real business results. Today, I'd love to understand your goals and see if we're a good fit to work together. I'll ask some questions, share some ideas, and if it makes sense, we can discuss next steps. Sound good?"

---

**PART 2: DISCOVERY QUESTIONS (15 minutes)**

**About Their Business:**
1. "Tell me about your business. What do you do?"
2. "Who are your ideal customers?"
3. "What makes you different from competitors?"
4. "What are your main business goals for the next 6-12 months?"

**About Their Current Situation:**
5. "Do you currently have a website? What's working and what's not?"
6. "What's the biggest challenge you're facing right now?"
7. "How are you currently getting customers?"
8. "What happens when someone visits your website?"

**About The Project:**
9. "What made you decide to reach out now?"
10. "What does success look like for this project?"
11. "If we could wave a magic wand, what would your ideal solution do?"
12. "Are there any websites you really like? What do you like about them?"

**About Timeline & Budget:**
13. "Do you have a timeline in mind for launch?"
14. "What's your budget range for this project?"
15. "Who else is involved in the decision-making process?"

---

**PART 3: SOLUTION PRESENTATION (7 minutes)**
- Summarize what you heard
- Present recommended solution
- Share relevant case study
- Explain your process
- Mention timeline and ballpark pricing

**Script:**
"Based on what you've shared, here's what I'm thinking... [Present solution]. We've done similar projects for [case study]. Our process takes about [X weeks], and projects like this typically range from $[X] to $[Y]. Does that align with what you had in mind?"

---

**PART 4: NEXT STEPS (5 minutes)**
- Answer their questions
- Address any concerns
- Explain proposal process
- Set timeline for proposal
- Schedule follow-up if needed

**Script:**
"Great! Here's what happens next: I'll put together a detailed proposal with pricing options and send it to you by [date]. Then we can schedule a quick call to go through it together. How does that sound?"

---

### POST-CALL ACTIONS
- Send thank you email immediately
- Send proposal within 24-48 hours
- Add to CRM/follow-up system
- Schedule follow-up reminder
